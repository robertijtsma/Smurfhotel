We're Hiring!

Do you think you can join our team?

Hello, Eregit

It's that time of year where we're looking to expand our management team. If you think that you would make a good addition to this team, then read on because this article is for you.

Hybrid Hotel is one of the few hotels that strives to create an enjoyable environment that is safe for each and every user. We do this by ensuring our staff have the correct training in order to deal with all types of situations. However, as Hybrid Hotel grows (thanks to you guys), we must ensure that we have an adequate amount of staff members to moderate the hotel.

Being a member of the hotel staff is a time consuming and difficult job. We require that applicants be dedicated, fair, honest and kind to users of Hybrid Hotel. The staff positions we are looking to fill out are:

  • Community Leader (x1)
  • Trial Moderators (x2)

If you think that you are capable of becoming a member of staff, then you can apply by filling out the form that accompanies this article. After you have applied, we will send a confirmation email to your selected email address to let you know that we have recieved your application. We advise that you add support@hybridhotel.uk to your contacts to make sure that you receive our e-mails.
